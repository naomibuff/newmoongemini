The Cosmic Weather

What Is Happening in the Sky

On Sunday 14 June 2026, we have a New Moon in Gemini — and not just any New Moon. This is a Super New Moon, the second of five supermoons this year, which means the moon is sitting closer to Earth than usual, amplifying everything this lunation is here to activate. You will feel this one.

Supermoon Medicine

Supermoons bring the lunar energy closer — more potent, more felt, more immediate. Your intentions carry extra weight tonight. Your words carry extra power. Meet this moon with presence, not pressure.

Gemini is an air sign ruled by Mercury, the planet of mind, voice, communication and perception. This is the sign of the cosmic messenger — curious, quicksilver, alive to possibility and the spaces between things. And with Black Moon Lilith sitting opposite in Sagittarius, there is a wild, untamed edge to this lunation, asking you to stop settling for anything that no longer fits the woman you are becoming.

This is also the last New Moon of spring. As the summer solstice draws close, the cosmos is asking you a powerful question: what seeds of intention do you want to carry with you across the threshold into a new season?

The Energy

Main Themes of This Moon

🌬️ Voice & Expression

Gemini governs the throat, the breath, the spoken word. This moon is activating your voice — your right to be heard, to speak your truth, to begin the conversations you have been putting off with yourself and with life.

🌱 The Inner Narrative

What story are you telling yourself about who you are and what is possible for you? This Gemini New Moon shines a light on the thoughts running like background programmes — and invites you to begin writing a new one.

🔮 Curiosity as Medicine

Gemini energy is naturally curious, playful, open. This moon invites you to soften the grip of certainty and meet yourself — and your life — with fresh eyes. What becomes possible when you stop knowing and start wondering?

New moons are always portals for planting seeds — and with Supermoon energy amplifying this one, the soil is exceptionally fertile. Get intentional. Get clear. What are you choosing to call in and grow from here?

🤝 Connection & Community

The Twins remind us we are not built for isolation. Gemini lights up our circles, our kindred spirits, the women who see us clearly. Who are you ready to reach toward? Who do you want to welcome into your world?

🦁 Lilith's Wild Edge

Black Moon Lilith in Sagittarius opposes this lunation with her fierce, sovereign energy. She does not shrink. She does not ask for permission. Her invitation to you: stop settling for anything that does not honour the woman you truly are.

Your Inner World

  • A restless, busy mind — thoughts coming quickly, ideas sparking, the sense that something wants to shift but you are not quite sure what
  • An urge to speak — to say something you have been holding back, to write, to reach out, to begin a conversation with yourself that has been waiting
  • Clarity arriving in flashes — moments of sudden knowing or insight cutting through the mental noise

  • Heightened sensitivity to the words you use about yourself and your life — old narratives feeling louder, or suddenly less true than they once did

As Gemini's ruling planet, Mercury governs how we think, speak, and receive information. With the moon in Mercury's sign, our inner dialogue is especially powerful right now — what you say to yourself matters deeply.

Soul Work

New Moon Journal Prompts

Find a quiet space, light a candle, and give yourself permission to write without editing. These prompts are for you and only you — let what wants to come, come.

  • If this new moon were a seed you were planting, what would you want it to grow into by the time the season turns? Let yourself be specific.
  • What do you want to say, create, or express in this next chapter of your life — and what has been stopping you from beginning?
  • Where in your life are you ready to get curious rather than certain — to explore rather than control?
  • What new conversation wants to begin — with yourself, with someone else, or with life itself?
  • What does the woman you are becoming need to believe about herself in order to step fully into the next season?
  • What does your mind need right now to feel nourished rather than overwhelmed?
  • What kind of connection and community do you want to call more of into your life?
  • Write your intentions for this lunar cycle beginning with: "I am calling in..." Let yourself go as long as feels true.
Moonie Witchy Ritual

Your Gemini New Moon Ritual

A ritual for voice, intention and planting seeds under the Supermoon ✦

You Will Need

  • A yellow, gold or white candle
  • Paper and a pen
  • A small mirror (optional)
  • Somewhere safe to keep your intentions
  • Your chosen crystal (optional)
  • Mint, lavender or frankincense (optional)
1

Set Your Space

Find somewhere quiet where you will not be disturbed for at least 20 minutes. Clear the energy with a few deep breaths or a moment of intentional stillness. Light your candle. Place your mirror in front of you if you have one. Hold your crystal if you are using one. This is your sacred container.

2

Open the Portal

Take three slow, conscious breaths — in through your nose, out through your mouth. Feel your feet on the floor. Feel your body arrive here, now. Say aloud or in your mind: "I am here. I am open. I am ready to receive and to plant."

3

Write Your Seeds

On your paper, write your intentions for this lunar cycle and the season ahead. Begin each one with "I am calling in..." or "I choose to grow..." and write freely, without editing. Let Gemini's curious, quicksilver energy move through your hand. There is no wrong answer here — only what is true and alive in you right now.

4

Speak Them Into Being

Read your intentions aloud — this step is essential under a Gemini moon. Mercury governs your voice, and your spoken words are seeds with this lunation. If you have a mirror, look into your own eyes as you speak them. Let yourself be the first person to truly witness and receive what you are calling in. Your voice is the magic here.

5

Seal and Keep

Fold your paper and place it somewhere intentional — under your candle tonight, on your altar, beneath your pillow, or tucked into your journal. This paper is not finished with yet. Return to it at the full moon in two weeks and witness what has already begun to move.

6

Close With Gratitude

Place your hands on your heart. Take three final breaths — slow, full, unhurried. Thank the moon, thank the night, thank yourself for choosing to show up for your own becoming. Blow out your candle with intention, or let it burn safely down. Carry the energy of tonight's seeds with you as you move into the days ahead.
